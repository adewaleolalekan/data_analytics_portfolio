Client Overview

Founded in 2010, Social Buzz is a fast-growing social media platform with over 500 million monthly active users. Their unique model prioritizes content performance over influencer presence, allowing users to remain anonymous. With a massive daily volume of unstructured data, the company needed expert advisory to scale its data infrastructure and prepare for an IPO.

Project Objective

Analyze platform content across 3 months to identify the top 5 most engaging content categories, providing actionable insights to guide Social Buzz's content strategy ahead of its IPO.

Data & Methodology

Datasets Used

Three key datasets were identified as essential: Content, Reactions, and ReactionTypes. Popularity is quantified by a Score associated with each reaction type, so linking categories to reaction scores was the core analytical task.

01
Cleaning the Content Dataset

Removed irrelevant URL and User ID columns. Standardised the Category column by stripping inconsistent quotation marks (e.g. "animals" vs animals) using Find & Replace. Renamed the Type column to Content Type for clarity.

02
Cleaning the Reactions Dataset

Removed the User ID column and deleted rows with blank values to maintain data integrity before merging.

03
Merging into a Final Dataset

Used the Reactions dataset as the base. Applied VLOOKUP to join Content Type and Category from the Content dataset (keyed on Content ID), then joined Sentiment and Score from ReactionTypes (keyed on reaction type).

04
Calculating Popularity Scores

Extracted a distinct list of all categories, then used SUMIF to aggregate the total Score per category — producing a clear, quantitative measure of popularity for ranking.

Key Insights

🐾 Animal Content Reigns Supreme

With the highest engagement score, animal-related content is a powerful driver of user interaction. Expanding this category offers a significant growth opportunity.

🚀 Science & Healthy Eating Are Rising Stars

Educational and health-focused content is rapidly gaining traction — developing more content here will likely boost engagement and attract new users.

📊 Technology & Food Are Stable Performers

These categories show consistent engagement. Innovating with new formats — tech trend analysis or interactive recipe challenges — could drive even more activity.

Summary & Recommendations

The data clearly shows that a diversified content strategy is key. By capitalizing on leading categories, investing in rising stars, and innovating on stable performers, Social Buzz can significantly enhance user engagement and position itself for a strong IPO.
